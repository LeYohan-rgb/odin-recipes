SHRIMP SALAD

INGREDIENTS

• 4 eggs (hard-boiled)

• 250g shrimp (9 oz or may use frozen baby shrimp)

• 1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple

• 5 ounces mayonnaise

• 3 ounces cream cheese (softened)

• 1/2 teaspoon paprika

• 1 teaspoon lemon juice

• 1 dash Tabasco sauce

• salt and pepper (to taste)

• 2 tablespoons chives (freshly-snipped)

STEPS

1. Chop eggs & shrimp as desired.

2. Combine all ingredients & mix well.

3. Refrigerate at least 2 hours before use.

4. NOTE: Serving sizes will vary & depend upon whether used as a dip/spread or a sandwich filling.
I reflected 2 oz appy servings & would expect 8 sandwiches using 3 oz servings.
